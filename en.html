About us

A fleet that's grown without pause since 2020

Bies Lojistik Gıda Sanayi ve Ticaret Ltd. Şti. set out in Adana, Türkiye in October 2020 with bulk tanker (silo) transport. Each year we added a new line of service, expanding our fleet and service points.

Growing fast, we continue to widen our network as a logistics solution partner to Türkiye's leading producers.

OCTOBER 2020

Bulk tanker transport

We launched logistics operations from Adana with bulk tanker (silo) transport.

JANUARY 2022

Dry freight with curtain-side trucks

We moved into dry-freight transport with a curtain-side truck fleet.

NOVEMBER 2023

Cold chain transport
