The cold chain, unbroken

From −20°C to +25°C,
without a single gap

Every reefer is calibrated, pre-cooled and logged. Sensors stream temperature the whole way, and our operations desk is alerted the moment a load drifts — so it's corrected before it's a problem.

CHILLED
Meat and cold-stored goods held steady around −20°C.
FRESH
Dairy and fresh food held steady at 0°C to +4°C.
PRODUCE
Fruit, vegetables and sensitive fresh goods at +4°C to +8°C.
AMBIENT

About us

A fleet that's grown without pause since 2020

Bies Lojistik Gıda Sanayi ve Ticaret Ltd. Şti. set out in Adana, Türkiye in October 2020 with bulk tanker (silo) transport. Each year we added a new line of service, expanding our fleet and service points.

Growing fast, we continue to widen our network as a logistics solution partner to Türkiye's leading producers.

OCTOBER 2020

Bulk tanker transport

We launched logistics operations from Adana with bulk tanker (silo) transport.

JANUARY 2022

Dry freight with curtain-side trucks

We moved into dry-freight transport with a curtain-side truck fleet.

FEBRUARY 2022

Container transport

We added container transport to our service portfolio.

NOVEMBER 2023

Cold chain transport

We launched temperature-controlled cold-chain service with refrigerated trucks.

2026

Sakarya office opened
